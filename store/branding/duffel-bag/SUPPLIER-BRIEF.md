## Primary Placement — Front Panel

- **Location:** Front panel, upper-center (above lower pocket seam)
- **Content:** Swimmer icon + "SWIMBUDDZ" text (full lockup)
- **Max size:** 15 cm wide (supplier constraint)
- **Recommended bounding box:** 15 cm wide x 12 cm tall
- **Orientation:** Horizontal, level, centered on panel
- **Vertical position:** Slightly above panel midline

### Logo Specifications

- **Icon:** ~8-9 cm wide
- **Text:** "SWIMBUDDZ" in Montserrat Bold, all caps, wide letter spacing
- **Text width:** ~13-14 cm (within 15 cm limit)

### Colors

- **On black bags:** Teal/cyan icon (#1A5C6B to #2A9DAF gradient) + white text
- **On light bags:** Dark teal icon + dark text (or full dark version)

---

## Secondary Placement — Side Pocket (Optional)

- **Location:** Round end pocket, centered
- **Content:** Swimmer icon only (no text)
- **Size:** 4-5 cm diameter
- **Clear space:** 1 cm from pocket edge
- **Both sides:** Mirror placement on each end

---

## Branding Method Preference

1. **Heat transfer** — Best for detailed multi-color logos on PU leather
2. **Embossed/debossed** — Premium feel, single color only
3. **Screen print** — Cost effective for bulk orders

---

## Design Decisions (Reference)

- All-caps "SWIMBUDDZ" chosen over mixed case for athletic/sporty feel
- Font: Montserrat Bold with wide letter spacing
- Teal logo on black bag preferred over pure white (maintains brand color identity)
- Logo positioned above panel midline for best visibility when bag is set down or carried
