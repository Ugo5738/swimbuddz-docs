## 5. SAFETY, LIABILITY & INSURANCE

### 5.1 Duty of Care

Pool Partner is responsible for the structural safety, hygiene, and statutory compliance of the Facility. SwimBuddz is responsible for the conduct and supervision of its members, coaches, and program participants during SwimBuddz sessions.

### 5.2 Indemnification

Each Party indemnifies and holds harmless the other against losses arising from the indemnifying Party's negligence, wilful misconduct, or breach of this Agreement.

### 5.3 Insurance

- Pool Partner warrants that it maintains **public-liability insurance** with coverage of at least ₦**[AMOUNT]** for the duration of this Agreement and will provide a certificate of insurance on request.
- SwimBuddz maintains its own **operator liability insurance** covering its coaches and members during SwimBuddz-run sessions.
- Each Party is responsible for its own insurance premiums.

### 5.4 Incident Reporting

Any incident (injury, near-miss, water-quality issue, property damage) occurring during a SwimBuddz session must be reported between the Parties within **[24]** hours in writing, with a full written incident report within **[7]** days.

### 5.5 Minor Safeguarding

Where SwimBuddz Academy minors are present, both Parties commit to:

- No unescorted access to changing rooms by non-family adults.
- Lifeguard:swimmer ratio no greater than **[1:10]** for learners under 12.
- Guardian check-in/check-out procedures agreed between SwimBuddz and Facility staff.

## 6. CONTENT, MARKETING & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 6.1 Facility Imagery

Pool Partner grants SwimBuddz a non-exclusive, royalty-free licence to capture and use photographs and short-form video of the Facility for marketing, social media, and member communications, subject to:

- No content that identifies other (non-SwimBuddz) patrons without their consent.
- Pool Partner may request redactions or takedown of specific material with **[7] days'** notice.

### 6.2 Brand Mentions

- SwimBuddz may mention Pool Partner by name and display its logo in pool-partner listings, session descriptions, and periodic marketing.
- Pool Partner may mention SwimBuddz by name and link to SwimBuddz channels. Use of the SwimBuddz logo requires written approval.

### 6.3 No Claim to IP

Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership of any Party's intellectual property (trademarks, curriculum, training materials, member lists) to the other Party.

## 7. DATA & PRIVACY

### 7.1 Member Data

SwimBuddz is the data controller for all member personal data. Pool Partner shall not require, collect, or retain personal data of SwimBuddz members without written consent.

### 7.2 Attendance Sign-In

Where Pool Partner requires attendance records (e.g. for facility logs), SwimBuddz will provide an aggregate session count, not individual member identities.

### 7.3 Confidentiality

Commercial terms in §3, member data, and this Agreement in general are treated as confidential between the Parties for a period of **[3] years** from termination, except where disclosure is required by law.

## 8. TERM, TERMINATION & RENEWAL

### 8.1 Initial Term

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for **[12] months** ("Initial Term") unless terminated earlier under §8.3.

### 8.2 Renewal

At the end of the Initial Term, this Agreement renews automatically for successive **[6]**-month periods unless either Party gives **[60] days'** written notice of non-renewal.

### 8.3 Early Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement:

- **For cause** (material breach not cured within **[14] days** of written notice): effective immediately on notice of non-cure.
- **For convenience**: with **[60] days'** written notice.
- **Automatically**: if Pool Partner loses legal right to operate the Facility, loses required certifications, or ceases operations.

### 8.4 Effect of Termination

On termination:

- SwimBuddz honours any bookings already confirmed for the following **[30] days** at the existing rate.
- All unpaid invoices fall due within **[14] days**.
- Each Party returns or destroys confidential material of the other.
- Neither Party has any further obligation except those that by their nature survive termination (§5 Liability, §7 Confidentiality, §9 Dispute Resolution).

---

## 9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### 9.1 Good-Faith Discussion

Any dispute shall first be the subject of good-faith discussion between a senior representative of each Party, initiated within **[14] days** of written notice of dispute.

### 9.2 Mediation

If not resolved, the Parties agree to mediation by a mutually-agreed mediator seated in Lagos, Nigeria, before commencing litigation.

### 9.3 Governing Law & Jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The Parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Lagos State.

---

## 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 10.1 Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding use of the Facility and supersedes prior discussions.

### 10.2 Amendments

Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

### 10.3 Assignment

Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the other Party's prior written consent, except that SwimBuddz may assign to an affiliate or successor in a corporate restructuring on notice.

### 10.4 Notices

Notices under this Agreement are valid if sent to the addresses on page 1 by email (with read-receipt) or by courier with proof of delivery.

### 10.5 Severability

If any provision is held invalid, the remainder of the Agreement remains in effect.

### 10.6 No Waiver

Failure by a Party to enforce any provision is not a waiver of that Party's right to enforce it later.

### 10.7 Force Majeure

Neither Party is liable for failure to perform caused by events beyond reasonable control (natural disaster, government order, pandemic, major infrastructure failure). The affected Party will resume performance as soon as practicable.
