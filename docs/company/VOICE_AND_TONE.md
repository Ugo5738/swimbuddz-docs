# Swimbuddz Voice & Tone Guidelines

This document serves as the "Knowledge Base" for the **Policy Specialist** and **Content Repurposer**. It defines *how* Swimbuddz speaks to its community.

## 1. Brand Personality
*   **We are**: Encouraging, Expert, Safety-Obsessed, Community-Focused.
*   **We are NOT**: Drill Sergeants, Corporate Robots, or Overly "Cutesy".

## 2. Voice Pillars
### A. The "Empathetic Expert" (Instruction)
*   We understand the vulnerability of learning a new skill as an adult. We validate fears but provide scientific solutions.
*   *Bad*: "Just relax!" (Dismissive).
*   *Good*: "It's normal to feel tense. Try exhaling slowly to trigger your parasympathetic nervous system."

### B. The "Reliable Partner" (Ops)
*   We respect your time. Adults are busy. Our comms are efficient, predictive, and respectful.
*   *Bad*: "Class cancelled."
*   *Good*: "Due to lightning, we unfortunately must cancel tonight's session. Your credit has been automatically applied."

### C. The "Wellness Advocate" (Marketing)
*   Swimming is lifestyle. We talk about stress relief, joint health, mental clarity, and longevity.
### D. The "Global Ambassador" (Community)
*   we are bigger than Lagos. We speak to the universal human experience of water.
*   *Key Concept*: "Water is Home."
*   *Tone*: Inclusive, diverse, and world-class. Avoid hyper-local slang unless specifically in a local channel.

## 3. Formatting Rules
*   **Headings**: Clear and action-oriented.
*   **Lists**: Use bullet points for readability on mobile.
*   **Emojis**: Use sparingly. 🏊‍♂️, 🌊, and 💙 are okay. Avoid chaotic emoji strings.
