# Founder Network Plan

**Owner:** Daniel
**Cadence:** Reviewed quarterly
**Last updated:** 2026-04-29

The 30-target network plan for the next 18 months. The goal is to upgrade the founder's network from operator-heavy (coaches, pool owners, parents) to capital-and-distribution-heavy (investors, distribution partners, sport-tech operators, corporate wellness buyers) — the relationships that make the next stage of SwimBuddz possible.

**Working assumption:** Targets cluster around (a) capital, (b) distribution, (c) operator wisdom, (d) corporate wellness buyers, (e) sport-tech globally. Names listed where confidence is high; otherwise the role/firm is named and Daniel should research the specific person.

**Cadence target:** 1 outbound message per day, 5 days a week → ~250/year → ~25 substantive conversations → ~5 deep relationships.

## 90-day cadence

**Week 1–2 — Setup**
- Personal CRM (Notion or Airtable): one row per target, fields = name, firm, tier, intro angle, status, last touch, next action.
- Build-in-public schedule: 2 posts/week on LinkedIn/X documenting SwimBuddz operations & lessons.
- Corporate wellness 1-pager (PDF): one-page pitch for HRMs.

**Week 3–6 — P0 outreach**
- 1 cold message/day to P0 targets (5/week, 25 in this period).
- Goal: 5 substantive replies, 2 meetings booked.

**Week 7–9 — P0 follow-through + light P1**
- Convert P0 replies to calls/visits.
- Begin light engagement with P1s (comment on their public content, no asks yet).

**Week 10–13 — Review and recommit**
- Score the quarter on: # meetings, # follow-ups, # warm relationships built.
- Update this doc — promote/demote tiers, add new targets, remove stale ones.

---

## Rules of engagement

- **Be useful first.** Send 3 high-value intros/month before asking for anything.
- **One specific question, not "can we chat."** Specificity gets replies; vagueness gets ignored.
- **Asymmetric value.** Offer content collaboration, market intel, intros — not just "I want X."
- **Build in public.** The cheapest way to be approachable is to be visible doing the work.
- **No mass sequences.** Each message is bespoke. 250 bespoke > 2,500 templated.
- **Track everything.** A relationship you didn't write down is a relationship you'll forget to nurture.
